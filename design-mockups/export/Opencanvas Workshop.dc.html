netpulse-sol.com took 9 hours. Here is how

A full client landing page, live and shipped in a single working day. That speed did not come from prompting harder. It came from a method, worked in order, with most of the thinking done before a single line of code. In 90 minutes we walk you through it and build a landing page with you.

What you'll do

Live, from brief to browser.

We hand everyone the same brief and run the method end to end in front of you, in the order it has to happen. Most people open a tool and start prompting. We start somewhere else, and that is most of the reason the page ships in a day. You build alongside us, on the same brief, in the same 90 minutes.

Live build Build in real time alongside the host, on your machine, on the brief we provide.
The toolkit Claude Code, shadcn and 21st.dev, Vercel. What each one is for, and what you can skip.
Live Q&A Get unstuck while you build, not a week later.
The method, in the order that makes it work.
The toolkit: Claude Code for the build, shadcn and 21st.dev for the UI layer, Vercel to ship.
Prompts that hold up, and the moments you take the keyboard back.
Deploy, so the page ends up at a URL and not on localhost.

Honest note — Different machines, different agents, different usage limits. Some of you will finish in the room, some will be halfway. Both are fine. The method is the thing you came for, and you leave with everything you need to finish on your own time.
